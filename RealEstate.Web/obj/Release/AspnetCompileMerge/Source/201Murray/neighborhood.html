Outdoor

The part of the city that surrounds 201 Murray is a surpassing example of central Manhattan. These vibrant streets are intriguing with its richness of cultural institutions, elegant restaurants, park areas and the variety of shops.

Culture

This area is the cultural center of the city. Such majestic places as The Metropolitan Museum of Art, The Frick Collection, and The Guggenheim are located within walking distance from 201 Murray. Various exhibitions, art galleries, and museums give an exceptional opportunity to learn all the secrets of New York.

Shops

The neighborhood has one more distinctive feature. There are a large number of wonderful specialty stores there. It is hardly possible not to admire these unique storefronts. Even the most demanding customer will be definitely satisfied.

Dining

Besides the cultural institutions, 201 Murray has other neighbors as well. Luxury restaurants with different cuisines from around the world, classic bars and cafes are going to be useful for any case.
