Inside, Uutside – Elegant Giant
Focused On Details

201 Murray offers 39 exceptionally well-planned floors with 98 unique residences.
Anywhere you look it is surrounded by beautiful views. The straight streets filled with dynamic life
and cozy parks looking magnificent in any season are drawing in the windows stunning pictures.
Each residence has prepared something very individual for you.

Kitchen / Living Room

You will definitely fall in love with such a practical and superior well-equipped kitchen. It combines both as technical accomplishments and comfort, as style and beauty. There are gas cooktop, retractable downdraft hood, wine storage and many other necessary elements of the kitchen area.

Master Bedroom

This room invites you to the atmosphere of harmony and satisfaction. Most of the master suites have lovely balconies to enjoy early morning or sunset time.

Master Bathroom

Quite roomy master bath offers an ample tub covered with posh marble. Relax and relieve the stress at the end of the day.

Child Rooms

There is an amazing secret world of games and adventures behind the doors of every child room. Comfortable and safe furniture, multi-paned double-hung windows frame, multiple zones of heating and cooling – all these make this place the best one for children's relaxation and fun.

Secondary Bathroom

Wide bathroom with great design shower area.

Private Balcony/Terrace

Enjoy the silence and fresh morning air with a cup of coffee on the balcony or terrace.
